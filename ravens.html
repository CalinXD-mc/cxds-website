Ravens

Ravens are the new Mob that the mod adds. They can be found in Forests and they're tamed with seeds.

Using the Ravens

The Main use of the Raven is it's delivering capabilities. Right click a Raven with a Bundle that has the name of another Online player, and the Raven will deliver the bundle.

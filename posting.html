SillyMC & other more? (click me)

SillyMC is a personal project of mine that tries to make a enjoyable modded experience on newer Minecraft versions! After about 3 weeks it's finally up and I believe it's good enough to be public. If you were searching for a new and modern modpack, maybe try mine! (though I wonder how you found this website first lmao). Initially it was supposed to be called SillySMP, but there was already a SillySMP so I rebranded it about an hour before it was approved.
